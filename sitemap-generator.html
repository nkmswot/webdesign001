What is an XML Sitemap?

An XML sitemap is a file that lists all pages on your website to help search engines like Google and Bing discover and index your content faster. Upload the generated sitemap.xml to your website root directory and submit it via Google Search Console under Sitemaps. It is especially important for new websites or pages with few internal links.
