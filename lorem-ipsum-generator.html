What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem ipsum is placeholder text that has been used by typesetters and designers since the 1500s. The text derives from Cicero's de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum, scrambled to appear as meaningless Latin. It lets designers focus on layout and typography without being distracted by readable content.

Paragraphs, Sentences, or Words?
