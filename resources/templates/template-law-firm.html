The Firm

A Legacy of
Principled Counsel

Founded in 1978 by Thomas Ashworth and Richard Cole, the firm has earned its reputation one case at a time.

Ashworth & Cole was founded on a straightforward conviction: that clients deserve lawyers who combine genuine legal excellence with the kind of clear, honest communication that makes difficult situations navigable.

For over four decades, we have held to that standard. Our attorneys are recognized among the best in their fields — not because of marketing, but because of results. We have represented Fortune 500 companies and individuals alike, and we bring the same preparation and commitment to every matter.

We are not the largest firm in the city. We are, by deliberate choice, a firm where partners know their clients by name and where every matter receives senior attention.

Common Questions

Before You Call

Answers to questions we hear most often from prospective clients.

Initial consultations last 45–60 minutes and are conducted by a partner. We will review the facts of your matter, explain the relevant law, discuss potential strategies, and give you an honest assessment. There is no charge for the first consultation.
Our fees depend on the nature of the matter. Transactional and planning work is billed at hourly rates or fixed fees. Litigation matters may be handled on contingency where appropriate. We provide a clear engagement letter before any work begins.
We are licensed in New York, New Jersey, and Connecticut. For federal matters and multi-state transactions, we regularly work with counsel in other jurisdictions. Please contact us to discuss the specifics of your situation.
Timelines vary enormously by matter type and complexity. A simple will can be completed in days; a contested litigation may take years. At your consultation, we will give you the most honest timeline estimate we can based on the specifics of your situation.
All matters are supervised by a partner and most client communication is handled at the partner level. On larger engagements, associates assist with research and drafting under close partner oversight. You will always know who is working on your matter.
