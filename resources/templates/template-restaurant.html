Our Story

Food that starts
a conversation

Ember & Ash was born from a simple belief: that the best meals are the ones you remember. Our open wood-fired hearth is the heart of everything — from the slow-charred prime cuts to the hand-made pastas that take three days to perfect.

Chef Marcus Reid spent a decade cooking in Paris and New York before returning home to create something entirely his own. The menu changes with the season, honoring local farmers and what the land provides.

Marcus Reid
Executive Chef & Founder
