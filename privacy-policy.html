Privacy Policy

Effective: 1 January 2025

📅 Last updated: 1 January 2025

1. Introduction

WebDesign001.net operates a free collection of web design and development tools. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding it.

2. What We Collect

Tool input data: Not collected. All tool processing happens in your browser. Code, images, and generated documents are never sent to our servers.

Server logs: Our hosting provider automatically logs standard access data including IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, and pages visited. This is used in aggregate to understand traffic patterns.

Cookies: We use Google Analytics (anonymised usage data) and Google AdSense (advertising). You can control cookies through your browser settings.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve our tools and website
  • To understand how visitors use the site through aggregated analytics
  • To display relevant advertising via Google AdSense
  • To respond to contact form submissions
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

Google Analytics: Anonymised visitor data. Opt out via Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google AdSense: Contextual advertising. Opt out of personalised ads at Google's Ads Settings.

Google Fonts: Font requests are logged by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA you have rights under GDPR: access, correction, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. Contact us via the Contact page to exercise these rights.

6. Data Retention

Server logs are retained for 90 days. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months. We hold no database of user-entered tool content.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes are indicated by updating the date above. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact
