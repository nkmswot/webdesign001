Free QR Code Generator

Create QR codes for any purpose — websites, contact details, Wi-Fi credentials, and more. All generation happens entirely in your browser using a pure JavaScript QR encoder. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

QR Code Types

  • URL — links to any website or app
  • Wi-Fi — lets people join your network by scanning
  • vCard — shares contact info with any smartphone
  • Email / Phone — opens the mail or phone app when scanned

Error Correction Levels

Higher error correction lets the QR code remain scannable even if part of it is damaged or covered. Level M (15%) is recommended for most uses. Use Level H (30%) if you plan to overlay a logo on the QR code.
