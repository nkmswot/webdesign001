Free Color Picker & HEX to RGB Converter

WebDesign001's Color Picker lets you pick any color visually and instantly converts it to HEX, RGB, and HSL formats. Adjust colors with precision using the RGB and HSL sliders, check WCAG accessibility contrast ratios, and copy any value with one click.

How to Convert HEX to RGB

Enter any HEX color code (e.g. #6366f1) in the HEX field and the tool instantly converts it to RGB and HSL. You can also type directly into the HEX field and press Enter to update all values.

WCAG Contrast Ratio Checker

The contrast checker uses the WCAG 2.1 luminance formula to calculate the contrast ratio between your text color and background color. A ratio of 4.5:1 passes AA for normal text, and 7:1 passes AAA.
